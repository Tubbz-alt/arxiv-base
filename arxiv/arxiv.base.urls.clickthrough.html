arxiv.base.urls.clickthrough module¶

Functions to create hashes for clickthrough links.

These are used to log to the web acccess logs when the DOI and bookmarking links are clicked.

The hash is used to prevent malicious use of the click through controller to create links that look like they are on arXiv but get redirected someplace undesirable.
